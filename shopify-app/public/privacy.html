Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Who we are

AlgoVoi is a cryptocurrency payment platform operated by iLoveChicken.co.uk ("we", "us", "our"). This policy covers the AlgoVoi Shopify App.

What data we collect

When a merchant installs the AlgoVoi Shopify App, we collect and store:

  • Shop domain — your .myshopify.com address
  • Shopify access token — used to read and update orders on your behalf
  • AlgoVoi credentials — your Tenant ID and API key (entered by you during setup)

This data is stored in Cloudflare Workers KV, encrypted at rest.

What data we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store, or process any customer personal data, including:

  • Customer names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Billing or shipping addresses
  • Payment card details
  • Browser fingerprints or tracking data

Order data (amounts, order numbers) is processed transiently to create payment links but is not stored by AlgoVoi.

How we use your data

  • Create cryptocurrency payment links for your customers' orders
  • Update order notes with payment links
  • Mark orders as paid when payment is confirmed on-chain
  • Verify webhook signatures for security

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Payment transactions are processed on public blockchains (Algorand, VOI, Hedera, Stellar) which are inherently transparent.

Data retention

Merchant credentials are retained while the app is installed. When you uninstall the app, Shopify sends a shop/redact webhook and we delete all stored data for your shop within 48 hours.

Data security

  • All communication uses HTTPS/TLS
  • Webhook signatures verified via HMAC-SHA256
  • Access tokens stored in Cloudflare Workers KV (encrypted at rest)
  • No customer PII stored at any point

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data. Contact us at the address below.

GDPR compliance

We implement all three mandatory Shopify GDPR webhooks:

  • customers/data_request — we confirm no customer data is stored
  • customers/redact — we confirm no customer data to delete
  • shop/redact — we delete all merchant data from our systems
